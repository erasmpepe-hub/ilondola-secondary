About the School

Ilondola Secondary School, located in the heart of Shiwang'andu District, Muchinga Province, is a remarkable institution dedicated to providing quality education.

The school offers three specialized career pathways:

  • Business Studies: Financial management and entrepreneurial skills.
  • Social Sciences: Careers in law, governance, and community development.
  • Physical Education: Athletics and health sciences.
